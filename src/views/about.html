Who We Are

SIDASOFT Technologies is a premier IT training institute dedicated to bridging the skills gap in the technology industry.

Founded in 2010, we have trained over 10,000 professionals who now work at leading tech companies around the world. Our practical approach to teaching and industry-relevant curriculum sets us apart from traditional educational institutions.

We believe in learning by doing, which is why our courses emphasize hands-on projects and real-world applications.
