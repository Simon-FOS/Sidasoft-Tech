Who We Are

SIDASOFT Technologies is a forward-thinking ICT hub dedicated to bridging the digital skills gap in Nigeria's technology ecosystem.

As a new and innovative technology training center, we are committed to providing cutting-edge education that prepares students for the rapidly evolving tech industry. Our practical approach to teaching and industry-relevant curriculum sets us apart from traditional educational institutions.

We believe in learning by doing, which is why our courses emphasize hands-on projects and real-world applications. Our state-of-the-art facilities and industry-experienced instructors ensure that our students receive the highest quality education.
